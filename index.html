Staffing Solutions

When we visit a schools or college or a hospital, we are greeted by the front office staff checking our purpose of visit, and volunteering to assist us. Similarly, in our visits to a shopping mall, we are greeted by the boys or girls and enquiring how they could help us to make our visit purposeful. Likewise, when we visit a movie theatre, we go to booking counter (if we have not purchased tickets online) to purchase tickets from the person issuing the tickets.

The people whom we meet and interact at the schools or colleges or hospitals are front office personnel. Similarly, at shopping malls, the boys or girls greeting us are the sales personnel.

Service sector is in a surge creating more opportunities. However, hiring a right talent is emerging as a difficult task. Even though the placement agencies may provide some resumes, as they are not pre-screened, a lot of time is consumed before shortlisting the right talent. Even after hiring the right talent, if there is an opportunity elsewhere, people are switching over and the process again starts.

In such a scenario, a professionally managed staffing solutions entity plays a pivot role in pre-screening the right talent, before facilitating the data to the prospective employers.

White-Collar:

  • Schools: Teachers, Accountants, Administrative Staff, Front Office Staff, library assistants, physical training teachers/sports coaches
  • Colleges: Lecturers, Accountants, Front Office Staff, Administrative Staff, Wardens, library assistants, physical education/sports coaches
  • Hospitals: Trained Nurses, Lab Technicians, X-Ray-ECG-2D-TMT-OT technicians, Front Office Staff, Accountants, storekeepers
  • Shopping Malls: Sales Boys/Girls, Sales Supervisors, Assistant Managers, Marketing Staff, Cashiers
  • Movie theatres: Booking Clerks, Projector room operators, Supervisors, Assistant Managers, Managers

Blue-Collar:

  • Lab attendants, security guards, bus and van drivers, cleaners, gardeners, cooks, watchman, ambulance drivers, gardeners, attenders, and sweepers. We provide qualified, skilled and semi-skilled manpower.

Employer

We support the employers in meeting their recruitment and staffing requirements. Depending on the staffing and sub-staff needs, we provide the data of the prospective candidates to the employers for shortlisting. We are mostly confident of our data, which is twice pre-screened, and credentials are verified, thus saving the employers precious time and cost. We provide the following staff and sub-staff for the employer needs:

As per your requirements, we provide you the manpower at a moderate fee for the service and ensure that they comply with your needs. However, if the employee leaves within six months from their date of joining, we shall replace without a service charge.

How it works :

As per your staffing requirements, we provide you a list of the prospective candidates from our databank in the ratio of 1:3. The prospective candidates are thoroughly pre-screened twice and credentials viz. educational qualifications, experience if any, address attitude and aptitude, skills, competencies, and proficiency in soft-skills, for final shortlisting and to issue an offer letter, as per your HR policies.

On the day the employee joins the job, the employer will pay 50% of the salary fixed to the employee, as our service fee. Further, the employer will also deduct 50% from the employee’s fixed salary payable in the 1st month. The amounts payable by the employer as their service charge and the amount deducted from the employee shall be credited to the account of Just Join (JJ) Multi-Services (P) Ltd.

If the employee leaves the organization within six months from the date of joining either on disciplinary grounds or for better prospects, a free replacement of the suitable candidate shall be facilitated, without any service charges to the employer. However, the employer again has to deduct 50% from the first month’s fixed salary of the new employee and remit it to Just Join (JJ) multi-service (P) Ltd account.

Employee

Securing an employment close to your place of residence sometimes warrants an effort and fortune. Just join multi-services company through its network of contacts, would facilitate employment to the people who are qualified, skilled, semi-skilled but hardworking by nature. We provide you an appropriate placement with an assurance that you remain in the employment by being meticulous, sincere to the job, disciplined and obedient by nature and soft-spoken.

Just Join will conduct free registration camps to enroll the prospective staff and sub-staff to provide placement in reasonably good companies. For the purpose, we conduct a thorough pre-screening which includes:

  • Educational Qualifications
  • Experience Documents, if any
  • Address proof (aadhar/election ID/Bank pass-book/electricity bill/passport)
  • Date of birth certificate
  • Candidate's soft-skills
  • Aptitude and attitude of the candidate
  • Mock-interview

The prospective employees, who wish to enroll to the free registration, should fulfill the following additional requirements:

  • The prospective candidate should be a minimum literate. Further the candidate should have a minimum educational qualification as required for the job.
  • Should be reasonably in healthy condition
  • Minimum age is 18 years and above, but not exceeding 55 years of age.
  • Gents if needed be willing to work in the night shifts, depending on the nature of the employment, especially in hospitals, pharmaceutical outlets, shopping malls, movie theatres, and MSME companies, and hotels.
  • Women depending on the nature of employment should be able to work between 6am-8pm shifts, except in hospitals, where it may warrant working on rotational basis, round the clock.

Once we store the details of the prospective employees, we share the data bank to the prospective employers and arrange employment interviews to the prospective candidates at least more than 3 interviews for possible selection at schools, colleges, hospitals, shopping malls, movie theaters, and MSME companies with good working environment, who remits the monthly salary in time by a fixed date. However, the selection will be at the discretion of the employer. After the selection, once the joining date is decided by the prospective employer, an offer letter would be sent to the selected candidates.

After the employee joins the service, employer would deduct 50% from their 1st month fixed salary and remit to the account of Just Join (JJ) Multi-Services (P) Ltd, as a token of facilitation with the employment.

Realestate

With the stead fall in the interest rates on housing loans and the central government’s subsidy on housing, the demand for the property is steadily on the rise. Several people below the age of 32 are making steady investments on lands, apartments, villas and farm houses. Added to the natural demand, due to governmental policies, there is also a speculative demand resulting in rise in land values even in tier-III cities and suburban areas. However, few unethical practices are pushing the investors in unending litigations and embarrassing moments.

At this backdrop, Just Join with its prowess in property related matters offers its services in identifying the properties comprising lands, villas, gated community projects, and apartments at upmarket places for secured investments and decent appreciation on the property.

Just Join (JJ) in its endeavor ensures the prospective purchasers of the properties that shall the properties that are facilitated for sale purpose are thoroughly screened, legally cleared without any encumbrance, and litigation free. Further, the prices are as per the prevailing market rates without any markup in the values. Just Join (JJ) team is thorough professionals in the whole process. The properties available for sale are agricultural lands, open plots, independent houses, villas, and apartments, in the possession of the absolute owners. Just Join (JJ) process is completely transparent, and the whole deal takes place between the prospective buyer and owner in a meeting facilitated by us.

Pre-owned car sales

The year 2020 in the recent history enlightened the people on the importance of health, amidst the global COVID-19 pandemic, and spending on health, safety and comfort. These factors are now prompting the people to acquire pre-owned cars for commutation of the self and family, than a two-wheeler.

Just Join (JJ) facilitates the sale of pre-owned cars in the segments of hatchbacks, sedans, MPVs, SUVs, luxury and crossovers. To maintain transparency in the sale, after the prospective buyer shortlists the vehicle to be purchased, Just Join (JJ) will facilitate a meeting between the vehicle owner and prospective buyer, ensuring a smooth transaction.

Just Join in its endeavor, offers the second-hand cars from the entry level to high-end models in different budgets. However, the price varies from model to model depending on the kilometers driven, and other features.

Before arranging on display of the cars, we ensure a thorough check by the qualified and certified car technicians on the engine condition, suspension, AC, tyres, and overall condition of the vehicle. Further, we also screen to make sure that there are no challans by the traffic police, no cases registered on the car, and display only the cars that has a clean image.
